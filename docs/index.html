Caring for Egypt's Most Vulnerable

We support needy Coptic families, orphans, widows, and university students in Egypt — through monthly aid, education support, and emergency relief. A Montreal charity, run entirely by volunteers since 1989.

What We Do

Three Ways Your Gift Reaches Egypt

Monthly Financial Aid

Regular monthly support to 7,150+ needy families, widows, and orphans — identified by clergy of the Coptic Orthodox Church. Disbursed every month without interruption.

Learn more

University Student Support

728+ students are currently receiving monthly support. Over the decades, thousands have graduated and found employment — lifting their entire families out of poverty for good.

Learn more

Emergency Relief

When crisis strikes — illness, disaster, or displacement — we mobilize quickly to provide emergency funds, medicine, and essentials to the families who need it most.
