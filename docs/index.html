Caring for Egypt's Most Vulnerable

The Coptic Aid Foundation supports needy Coptic families, orphans, and widows in Egypt — through monthly aid, small business projects, and emergency relief. Founded in Montreal. 100% volunteer.

What We Do

Monthly Financial Aid

We send regular monthly support to needy families, widows, and orphans selected by clergy of the Coptic Orthodox Church across Egypt.

Learn more →
[ICON or PHOTO: Students / university]

University Student Support

We support 728+ university students from low-income Coptic families — giving them the chance to build a future and lift their families out of poverty.

Learn more →
[ICON or PHOTO: Emergency / relief]

Emergency Relief

When crisis strikes, we mobilize quickly to provide emergency funds, medicine, and essentials to the most vulnerable.
