Get Involved

The Coptic Aid Foundation runs entirely on volunteer effort. Join us — your time and skills make a real difference.

Why Volunteer?

Every person on our board of directors, every administrator, every communicator — all volunteers. No one receives a salary or compensation. This means that when you give your time to the Coptic Aid Foundation, every hour goes directly toward serving families in Egypt.

"Therefore, knowing what is right to do and then not doing it, is sin." — James 4:17

How You Can Help

We welcome volunteers with a wide range of skills and availability. All roles are remote-friendly and flexible.

Communications

Social Media & Content

Help us tell our story — create content, manage social media channels, and share updates that connect donors to the families we serve.

Communications

Translation

Help us reach our full community by translating content between English, French, and Arabic — newsletters, website pages, and donor communications.

Technology

Web & Digital

Support the maintenance and development of our website, digital tools, and online donation infrastructure.

Administration

Family Aid Administration

Help manage and track the families we support — coordinating with field workers in Egypt to keep records current and aid flowing.

Finance

Accounting & Bookkeeping

Assist with financial record-keeping, donation tracking, and tax receipt preparation for our Canadian donors.

Events

Events & Fundraising

Help organize fundraising events, community gatherings, and outreach activities in the Montreal area and beyond.

Field Work

Egypt Field Support

If you are based in Egypt or travel there regularly, help us connect with communities, verify needs, and support our local representatives.

General

Other Skills

Have a skill not listed here? We are always open to new ideas. Tell us what you can offer — we will find the right fit.
