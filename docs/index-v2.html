What We Do

Three Ways Your Gift Reaches Egypt

Every donation goes directly to those we serve — no salaries, no overhead deductions.

✚
01

Monthly Financial Aid

Regular monthly support to 7,150+ needy families, widows, and orphans. Identified by clergy of the Coptic Orthodox Church. Disbursed every month without interruption.

University Student Support

728+ students receiving monthly support today. Over the decades, thousands have graduated and found employment — lifting their entire families out of poverty for good.

Emergency Relief
