How to Give

Option 1 — Credit Card or PayPal

Donate securely online using any major credit card or your PayPal account.

[BUTTON: PayPal Donate button — to be embedded. PayPal hosted button ID: XHJU69N8X8RLU]

You will be redirected to PayPal's secure payment page. You do not need a PayPal account to donate by credit card.

Option 2 — Interac e-Transfer (Canada)

Send an e-Transfer directly from your Canadian bank account — no fees, no third party.

  1. Log in to your online banking and select Interac e-Transfer.
  2. In the Recipient field, enter: Coptic Aid Foundation
  3. Enter the amount you wish to contribute.
  4. Select the account you want to send from (Savings or Chequing).
  5. Click Verify.
  6. Enter the recipient email: donate@copticaidfoundation.org
  7. In the Message field, include your full name, mailing address, email, and phone number. This is required to issue your tax receipt.
  8. Do not set a security question. Donations are deposited automatically into the Coptic Aid Foundation account.

Tax Receipts

The Coptic Aid Foundation is a registered Canadian charity. All donations are eligible for a Canadian federal and provincial tax receipt.

  • Receipts are issued annually and mailed to the address on file
  • For credit card / PayPal donations, ensure your mailing address is current
  • For e-Transfer donations, include your address in the message field
  • Questions about your receipt? Call us at (514) 334-9792

Frequently Asked Questions

Does my entire donation reach the families?

Yes. Our board of directors is entirely volunteer. No member receives a salary or compensation. Your donation goes entirely to the families we serve in Egypt.

Can I make a recurring monthly gift?

Yes. Through PayPal you can set up a recurring monthly donation. For Interac, you can set up a recurring e-Transfer through your bank. Contact us if you need help setting this up.

Can I sponsor a specific family?

Yes. Contact us at info@copticaidfoundation.org or call (514) 334-9792 to discuss family sponsorship.

What if I miss a month?

Families continue to receive their monthly aid even if a sponsor is late or unable to give. The Foundation ensures no family is left without support due to a donor delay.
